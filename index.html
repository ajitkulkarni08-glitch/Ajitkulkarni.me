About Ajit

A career built on human potential.

[ Your photo goes here ]

Ajit Kulkarni is the founder and principal coach with over 30 years in the Pharma industry, where he led Training & Development functions at some of India's most respected companies. He also served as an Internal Coach for senior and middle managers, logging over 2,000 hours of coaching.

A faculty member at Management Institutes for MMS programmes, Ajit brings both academic depth and real-world industry insight to every session. He has trained over 5,000 Pharma sales professionals and coached hundreds of corporate leaders — helping them grow not just in their careers, but in confidence, relationships, and happiness.

Over the last three years, Ajit has also extended his expertise to students and their parents — helping over 300 families navigate career choices using tools like DISC, 16PF, and DMIT. He conducts Public Speaking workshops for college students, and works one-on-one with professionals seeking growth and fulfilment.
